Why this matters: Stationary combustion is typically the largest Scope 1 source for manufacturing, logistics, and commercial real estate. Under the GHG Protocol Corporate Standard, all fuel burned in equipment you own or control must be reported as Scope 1 regardless of fuel type. Switching from coal to natural gas reduces emissions per kWh by approximately 50%; switching to grid electricity moves the emission to Scope 2 and enables renewable energy matching.

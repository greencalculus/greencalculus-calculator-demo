Why this matters: Under the GHG Protocol, only the fossil-derived CH₄ and N₂O from biomass/biofuel combustion is counted in Scope 1. The biogenic CO₂ released is reported as a separate memo item (outside the scopes), never added to the Scope 1 total — for wood fuels it is typically 20–40× larger than the in-scope figure. Switching a fossil boiler to biomass slashes the reported Scope 1 number, but the biogenic line keeps the carbon visible and auditable.
